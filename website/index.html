Insight 01 · Checkout · 34% dropout
Hidden shipping cost is your biggest leak
$6.99 shipping appeared at checkout without prior mention. Agents with high loss aversion — 52% of your panel — showed unexpected_cost friction the moment it appeared. Show shipping cost on the product page.
⚡ Test a fix
Insight 02 · All stages · 70% stalled
Stall epidemic — not a dropout problem
70% of your panel is interested but not buying. This isn't rejection — it's hesitation. The missing piece is price confidence. Add "Save $18 vs dining out" comparison text to the product page. Prospect Theory predicts this reframes the purchase as a gain.
Insight 03 · Segment split
West African familiar buyers need different copy
Agents with high West African cuisine familiarity drop on value — they can make this cheaper at home. Reframe copy from authenticity to time-saved and convenience. "Skip the 2-hour prep" outperforms "restaurant-quality" for that segment.
