Top insight from this run

Hidden shipping cost is your biggest leak

$6.99 shipping appeared at checkout with no mention on the product page. Customers with high loss aversion — over half this panel — hit unexpected cost friction and stalled or left.

Suggested fix: Show shipping on the product page, or offer free shipping on first orders.
